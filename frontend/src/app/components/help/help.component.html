Need Help?

Hello, if you are seeking help, look no further. This website was designed to assist physician assistant students find their perfect graduate school.

1. On the home page, simply enter your credentials. Hit 'Graph'.
2. On the next page, you are required to enter your name and email to get results.
3. Once that is completed, simply hit 'View Results'.

There will be three bell graphs presented on the next page. It will list the schools you are eligible to get into based on what you entered. You also have an option to view all schools nationally. You may also apply through the central server link. Enjoy!
